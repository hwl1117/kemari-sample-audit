Concept UI Case Study

Kinfolk House

A concept for a hospitality-led community venue that needs to feel memorable, welcoming, and simple to book from a first visit.

Independent concept work. Not commissioned client work or performance claims.

Project briefGive a multi-use venue a confident online identity while keeping events, memberships, and booking options easy to find.

FocusBrand personality, programme discovery, event conversion, and mobile booking.

RoleKemari Blakemore
Website UI Designer

Design Direction

Hospitality brands do not need to choose between character and usability. This direction uses colour and editorial rhythm to create a feeling, then gives visitors an obvious route to the programme that matters to them.

Personality in the openingA clear point of view before the visitor reaches a menu of options.
Programme as navigationEvents, spaces, and memberships organised by visitor intent.
Booking without frictionAvailability and group enquiries made visible on every key page.
