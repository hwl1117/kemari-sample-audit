Sample Website Audit

Riz & Mona Consultancy: consultation conversion review

A short sample audit showing how a corporate services website can guide more high-intent visitors toward a qualified consultation request.

Quick Summary

Riz & Mona already has a strong website foundation: clear positioning, visible consultation CTAs, a setup cost calculator, broad service coverage, and credibility signals.

The main opportunity is not a full redesign. The practical opportunity is to make the path from high-intent visitor to consultation more direct.

This sample audit focuses only on website structure, CTA clarity, forms, and visitor flow. It does not review UAE legal, tax, visa, banking, or company formation advice.

Key Visitor Intents

High-intent visitors may arrive with several different needs. The page can help them self-select faster before requesting a consultation.

Company FormationNew founders comparing business setup options.
Free Zone vs MainlandVisitors unsure which jurisdiction fits their activity.
Visa SupportFounders and investors asking about residency or Golden Visa routes.
Banking and TaxBusinesses needing bank account, accounting, VAT, or corporate tax support.

Findings

Finding 1

Calculator To Consultation Flow

Current risk: Visitors may use the setup cost calculator, browse several service areas, and leave without a clear next step.

Suggested improvement: After calculator interaction, route the visitor into a short consultation CTA based on business activity, jurisdiction interest, visa needs, and timeline.

Example CTA: Get a setup recommendation based on your activity, jurisdiction, and visa needs.

Finding 2

Service Path Segmentation

Current risk: The homepage and service sections cover many useful services, but different visitors may need different paths.

Suggested segmentation:

  • I want to form a company.
  • I need help choosing mainland vs free zone.
  • I need residency or investor visa support.
  • I need corporate bank account support.
  • I need tax or bookkeeping support.
Finding 3

Service-Level Trust Proof

Current risk: Strong trust proof is most useful when it appears near the specific service decision point.

Suggested improvement: Place relevant proof near the CTA for each high-intent service, such as banking process clarity, visa document preparation, free zone comparison logic, and tax/accounting review process.

This does not require unsupported claims. It means placing the right proof near the right action.

Suggested Mini Project

Recommended first engagement:

Calculator-to-consultation conversion audit
  • Review the current calculator and CTA path.
  • Map 3-5 visitor intent paths.
  • Recommend revised CTA copy and form fields.
  • Draft one improved high-intent landing page structure.

Suggested Form Fields

A business setup consultation form can qualify users earlier with fields such as:

  • Business activity
  • Preferred jurisdiction: mainland / free zone / not sure
  • Number of shareholders
  • Visa needs
  • Timeline
  • Bank account support needed
  • Accounting or tax support needed
